Vorliq

Your Community Your Coin

Vorliq is a self contained community savings platform built on its own blockchain. It lets people create wallets, send VLQ, mine blocks, and build a shared financial record without depending on an outside cryptocurrency network.

21M maximum VLQ supply
50 VLQ initial block reward

Features

Own Blockchain

Vorliq runs on its own lightweight proof of work blockchain. Blocks, transactions, wallets, balances, and mining all belong to the Vorliq application itself.

Real Cryptography

VLQ transactions use real SECP256K1 cryptographic keys and signatures. The chain verifies signed transactions before they can be mined into blocks.

Community Governed

Vorliq is built for community ownership. Future versions are designed to support voting on network rules and lending decisions through VLQ participation.

The VLQ Coin

VLQ is the native coin of the Vorliq network. It is used for transfers, balances, mining rewards, and future community lending activity. The total maximum supply is 21 million VLQ, and miners earn an initial reward of 50 VLQ for each block they mine.

21,000,000 VLQ maximum supply
