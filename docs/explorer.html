Block Explorer

Explore the live Vorliq blockchain, inspect blocks, search wallet activity, and understand the difference between pending and confirmed VLQ transactions. Common explorer reads are served through rebuildable derived indexes while chain.json remains canonical.

Pending vs confirmed

Pending transactions are accepted into the pool but are not final. They become confirmed when a miner includes them in a valid block.

Confirmations

A confirmed transaction gains confirmations as newer blocks are mined after its block. More confirmations mean deeper chain history.

Lookup tools

The app explorer supports direct routes such as /tx/TX_ID and /block/BLOCK_HASH for transaction and block detail pages.
