Complete Platform

Open tools for an experimental community chain

Own Blockchain

Vorliq runs on its own proof of work blockchain with signed transactions, mined blocks, persistent storage, and automatic validation. It does not depend on any external cryptocurrency network.

Community Lending

Members can request VLQ support and the community can approve or reject requests with balance-weighted voting. This is experimental software, not a licensed lending service.

Decentralized Exchange

Community members can post buy and sell offers for VLQ with flexible real-world pricing. This is an experimental offer board, not a licensed exchange.

Governance

VLQ holders can propose and vote on network rule changes. Approved proposals can automatically update supported settings such as mining reward and difficulty.

Mobile App

The React Native Expo mobile app brings wallet, sending, lending, exchange, governance, settings, and notifications to Android and iOS phones.

Peer Network

Vorliq nodes can register peers, discover other nodes, broadcast transactions and blocks, synchronize chains, and monitor network health.

The VLQ Coin

Native to Vorliq

VLQ is the native coin of Vorliq. It has a maximum supply of 21 million VLQ, a starting mining reward of 50 VLQ per block, and a halving schedule every 210000 blocks. VLQ has no guaranteed market value, and the community can vote through governance to change supported network parameters over time.
