Brand Guide

Vorliq

Vorliq should feel dark, trustworthy, modern, human, and technical without becoming cold. The visual language is deep navy and black, green/cyan/blue light, blockchain node patterns, glass cards, clear white type, and a visible community-first tone.

Typography

Use Inter with large clean headings, readable body text, generous spacing, and no cramped mobile layouts. Headline text may use green/cyan/blue gradient emphasis for VLQ or key action words.

Cards and Buttons

Cards use dark glassmorphism, thin cyan borders, and subtle glow. Buttons use rounded shapes, strong contrast, visible focus states, and clear action labels.

Responsible Tone

Vorliq is inspiring but honest. Public materials should describe VLQ as experimental, avoid guaranteed-value language, and explain that Vorliq is not a licensed bank, broker, exchange, lender, or investment adviser.

Logo

Use the existing Vorliq logo asset. Do not recreate or distort it. Keep enough spacing around it and avoid placing it on low-contrast backgrounds.
