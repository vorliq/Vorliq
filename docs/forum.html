Forum and Chat Safety

Public Content

Forum posts, replies, images, screenshots, and chat messages are public community content. Do not post private keys, passwords, recovery phrases, admin tokens, government documents, private contact details, or anything you would not want copied outside Vorliq.

Reports

Use Report buttons to flag spam, impersonation, abuse, scams, illegal content, or other problems. A report adds an item to the moderation queue. It does not automatically delete the post, reply, chat message, or profile.

Moderation

Moderators can hide content from normal lists, lock posts from new replies, and manage pinned or featured states. These controls are non-destructive and do not rewrite blockchain history.
